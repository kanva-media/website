Our Process

Strategy first.
Always.

Every Kanva Media engagement begins with research — three layers of it. We study your business positioning, how your audience consumes content, and what topics will actually perform. Only then do we plan your season.

Our edge: We are not a production house. We are a content strategy firm that works end-to-end — from research to clips. No other player in this space owns the full chain.

Phase 01 · Pre-Production

The Research Layer

Before a single topic is confirmed, we run three parallel research streams: Business Research (how your brand should be positioned on social media), Market Research (how your audience consumes content), and Content Research (what topics will attract, engage, and convert). This is what most podcast services skip entirely — and why most podcasts fail.

We curate 8 episode topics mapped to your audience's needs and business objectives. For each episode, we craft the exact questions to draw out the insights your audience came for. We also shortlist the right guests — because who you invite shapes how the content is received.
